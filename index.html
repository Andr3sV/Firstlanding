Your investment is made through the training rights of football clubs

We break down the Training Compensation or Solidarity Mechanism (FIFA) that clubs receive for nurturing established players. By acquiring a share, you can share in the profits from each player’s upcoming transfer.

Primary Market

Dive into Direct Player Action! Invest in a player’s journey by grabbing a ‘participation’ in their rights. When the player is transfered, your profits match your invested ‘participation’. Join the thrill of their rise to stardom – this is your VIP pass to the heart of football transactions!

Secondary Market

Level Up in the Trading Arena! Explore our secondary market where you can swap ‘participation’ with fellow fans. Ride the daily value waves influenced by demand. Sell your ‘participation’ at the right moment, and watch your profits soar. It’s more than a market; it’s a game-changer where your passion turns into winning moves.

Where do your invested fund go?

Funds raised from each share sale directly benefit the club that nurtured the player you invest in. Our mission is to empower clubs with enhanced resources, ensuring athletes’ growth by offering vital financial support to achieve their goals.
